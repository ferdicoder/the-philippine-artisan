ICYMI: TUP Manila celebrates newly licensed civil engineers

The Philippine Artisan • November 29 at 10:00 AM

TUP Manila recognizes the success of its 46 newly licensed civil engineers who passed the November 2025 Civil Engineering Licensure Examination (CELE).

With 46 out of 97 examinees succeeding, the university achieves a 47.42% passing rate, surpassing the national rate of 30.39% as announced by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC).

Congratulations, TUPians! The entire community is proud of you all.
