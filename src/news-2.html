LOOK: Mr. & Mrs. TUP system candidates partake in sash ceremony and tree planting

The Philippine Artisan • December 2 at 6:57 AM

Written by Yvie Padolina

Candidates from TUP Manila, TUP Taguig, TUP Cavite, and TUP Visayas gathered at the TUP Cavite Campus to receive their official sashes and participated in the tree planting activity last November 27, 2025. The activity themed “Tree Planting and Sashing Ceremony: Blooming the Wisdom of Sustainable Development and Climate Justice,” served as the official introduction of each candidate who will race for the Mr. & Ms. TUP System 2025 crowns.

TUP Cavite USG President, John Mars Soriano, officially opened the ceremony with his welcoming remarks, encouraging the candidates to let their advocacies shine. Afterwards, College Secretary of College of Science Dr. Mary Sheenalyn Rodil shared a message, emphasizing the importance of environmental stewardship which was read by John Nathan Baltezar, the event’s Vice Chairperson, on her behalf, “Let us make today the day we choose to stand for the Earth, plant for tomorrow, and leave a legacy of life that future generations will thank us for.”

As the program continued, Techno Theatro Band serenaded the TUPians with their performance which boosted the energy of the audience. After the intermission, the candidates introduced themselves and their respective colleges and campuses. VPAA Ryan Reyes led the sashing of the candidates. They also took the Oath of Environmental Protection symbolizing their pledge as a sign of their dedication in preserving and protecting the environment.

Mr. & Ms. TUP System 2025 Chairperson and TUPM USG Vice President Carvin James Galvez concluded the ceremony with a reminder that caring for the environment goes beyond the crowns and sashes. Right after the sashing ceremony, the candidates were given a chance to plant their own tree seedlings beside the TUP Cavite Library which reflects this year’s Mr & Ms. TUP System shared mission: to plant not just trees, but hope, purpose, and long-term commitment to sustainability.

The Coronation Night will be held at TUP Manila Main Campus on December 4, 2025
