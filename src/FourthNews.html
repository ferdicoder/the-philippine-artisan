LOOK: 9TH ADFA Sportsfest concludes at TUP Covered Court

The Philippine Artisan • November 29 at 5:00 PM

Written by Laurean Aquino and Beverly Jane Dasig

The 9th Admin and Faculty (ADFA) Sportsfest took place at the TUP Covered Court from November 25 to 27. Participants include Admin and faculty teams from all colleges, as well as TUP branches in Cavite and Taguig.

They compete in multiple sports under the theme “One goal, one team towards a healthy community through sports.”

In chess, CIT wins gold, COS takes silver, and CIE earns bronze. In basketball, Admin claims gold, Taguig takes silver, and COE wins bronze. In darts, Cavite wins both men’s and women’s gold, CAFA takes silver, and Admin earns bronze.

In table tennis, Cavite wins gold, CIE takes silver, and Admin secures bronze.

While in volleyball, Taguig wins both the men’s and women’s gold. Cavite takes men’s silver, Admin wins women’s silver, and CIT and CLA take bronze in men’s and women’s volleyball, respectively.

In badminton, Admin wins men’s gold, CLA claims women’s gold, CAFA and CIE take silver, and COE and Admin finish with bronze.

This year's ADFA ends with the TUP Admin as the overall champion, Cavite as the first runner-up, and Taguig as the second runner-up.
