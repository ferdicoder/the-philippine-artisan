ANNOUNCEMENT: Civilian attire and academic adjustments scheduled for Campus Youth Day

The Philippine Artisan • November 25 at 11:48 PM

Students will be allowed to wear civilian attire and receive academic workload adjustments on November 27 and 28, 2025. This is in accordance with the upcoming Campus Youth Day, as part of the celebration of TUP's 124th Foundation Anniversary.

The memorandum permits all students to wear civilian clothing during the two-day event, provided it follows the University Dress Code Policy.

Additionally, faculty members are asked to move examinations, major submissions, quizzes, and performance tasks originally scheduled for the given dates.

Attached in the comment section is the full memorandum. Please be guided, TUPians!
